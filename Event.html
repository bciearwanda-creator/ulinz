FAQs

Questions people ask before they register.

This section reduces confusion and helps participants trust the process.

Can I register as a team?

Yes. Students, families, companies, churches, and community groups can register as teams.

Will I receive a ticket?

Yes. The registration system should generate a QR code ticket for fast check-in on event day.

Can people outside Rwanda support?

Yes. The diaspora page will allow donations, merchandise purchases, memorial sponsorships, and team support from abroad.

Is the walk friendly for survivors and families?

Yes. The route should include support points, clear signage, volunteers, and health guidance.
