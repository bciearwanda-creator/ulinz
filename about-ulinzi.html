Our Story

From a walk to a national movement.

Ulinzi was created to make breast cancer awareness visible, personal, and community-owned.

2011

The first steps

Ulinzi begins as a community walk to raise awareness and bring people together around breast cancer education and survivor support.

2015

Community growth

The movement expands with more volunteers, partners, families, and health education activities connected to the walk.

2020

Beyond the event

Ulinzi becomes more than one day, connecting awareness to outreach, stories, support groups, and year-round fundraising.

2026

A digital movement platform

Ulinzi.org becomes a place to register, donate, shop wearables, volunteer, sponsor, learn, honor loved ones, and follow impact.

Mission & Vision

Built for awareness, support, dignity, and hope.

Mission

To unite communities, raise breast cancer awareness, support survivors, and create lasting impact through collective action, fundraising, innovation, and public education.

Vision

A Rwanda where every person understands breast health, every family knows where to seek support, and every survivor receives dignity, care, and hope.
