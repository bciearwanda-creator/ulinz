Common Questions

Before You Message Us

Most answers are available on the platform pages. Here are the most frequent questions.

Can I register a team?

Yes. Team and corporate registration pages allow multiple participants, shirt sizes, and a team leader contact.

Can diaspora supporters donate?

Yes. The platform supports international donations, memorial gifts, merchandise purchases, and survivor sponsorship.

Can my company sponsor Ulinzi?

Yes. Visit the sponsor page to review packages and submit interest for the sponsorship deck.

Can volunteers choose roles?

Yes. Volunteers can select roles such as registration, social media, health education, logistics, photography, and more.

What payment methods are supported?

MTN MoMo, Airtel Money, Visa/Mastercard, and PayPal for diaspora supporters.

Where is the event taking place?

Kigali, Rwanda. Exact venue details will be confirmed and published on the event page.
