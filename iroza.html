What is IROZA?

A digital bridge between awareness and action.

IROZA helps communities understand breast health in a simple, respectful, and accessible way — extending BCIEA's education far beyond a single event day.

Simple Health Education

Clear, compassionate answers about awareness, early detection, screening, survivor support, and where people can seek help.

Multi-Language Support

Built for Rwanda's real communication needs with Kinyarwanda, English, French, and future Swahili support.

Mobile-First Access

Designed for smartphones, WhatsApp interactions, QR codes, and fast access during outreach and campaign events.

Why it matters

Awareness must continue after the walk.

Ulinzi brings people together once a year. IROZA keeps educating all year — especially students, families, village ambassadors, and diaspora supporters.

Supports community ambassadors

Gives outreach teams a simple tool for common questions during village visits and campaigns.

Helps students learn

Young people can ask questions privately and understand how awareness protects families.

Reaches beyond Kigali

Supports rural education and people who cannot attend physical events.
