How the volunteer journey works.

Step 01

Apply Online

Submit your details, preferred role, availability, and WhatsApp number.

Step 02

Role Confirmation

The BCIEA team confirms your volunteer category and adds you to the right team group.

Step 03

Training Session

Attend a short orientation about Ulinzi, breast cancer awareness, event safety, and your assignment.

Step 04

Event Day Service

Serve with your team, welcome participants, support the movement, and help create a memorable walk.
